Design Proposal
for a better public plaza

The redesign introduces more planting, more places to sit, greater shade, and a clearer organization of pedestrian space. The goal is to improve both environmental comfort and everyday public use.

🌳

Planting Strategy

Added trees, planters, and green edges help reduce visual harshness and create a stronger sense of place.

🪑

Social Seating

Flexible seating supports resting, socializing, and daily use by different groups in the community.

☂️

Comfort + Shade

Shade structures and tree canopy improve comfort and make the site more usable for longer periods of time.

📍

Clearer Layout

Better spatial organization creates clearer pathways while still leaving room for gathering and pause.
