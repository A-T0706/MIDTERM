Site Research

Studying the existing conditions of Harlem Plaza and identifying opportunities for redesign.

View Proposal See Visuals

Research Findings
and Existing Conditions

This stage of the project focused on understanding how Harlem Plaza works now. I looked at comfort, circulation, visibility, seating, greenery, and the overall atmosphere of the plaza as a public space.

🌿

Limited Greenery

The site has room for more planting, shade, and natural elements that would make the environment feel softer and more inviting.

☀️

Exposure to Heat

Some parts of the plaza feel too exposed during warmer weather, reducing comfort and limiting how long people want to stay.

🪑

Need for Seating

More flexible and comfortable seating could support students, families, workers, and community gathering.

🚶

Circulation Issues

Better visual organization and clearer walking paths could improve movement and make the plaza easier to navigate.

Site Context

Mapping the surrounding streets and access points helped show how people move into and through the plaza throughout the day.
