Reimagining Harlem Plaza
as a stronger public space

This project studies the current plaza and proposes improvements through AI-assisted redesign, environmental thinking, and public-space planning. The main goals are to add greenery, increase comfort, improve seating, and create a more inviting community space.

🌿

More Greenery

New trees, planters, and landscape zones would soften the space and reduce heat.

🪑

Better Seating

Flexible benches and social seating clusters would support rest and gathering.

☀️

Shade + Comfort

Shade structures and planting can make the plaza more usable throughout the day.

🚶

Safer Movement

Clearer paths and stronger organization can improve circulation and comfort.

Current Conditions

The existing plaza has strong potential, but parts of it feel exposed, hard, and underused. Research focused on comfort, circulation, and atmosphere.

Learn More

Redesign Vision

The proposal introduces more planting, seating, shade, and stronger visual identity so the plaza feels more useful and welcoming.
