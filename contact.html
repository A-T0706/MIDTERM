Reflection

Through this project, I explored how design can improve a public space without removing its existing identity. Using research and AI-generated mockups helped me test possible changes and present a stronger final proposal.

The final redesign focuses on comfort, greenery, and community use, showing how thoughtful design can make a plaza feel more usable and welcoming.
